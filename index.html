AI Business Idea: Personalised Fitness and Nutrition Plans

This AI business focuses on creating personalised fitness and nutrition plans. It would use data such as age, weight, lifestyle, medical needs and goals to create custom workout and meal plans that adjust as the user progresses.

As someone who lifts weights and cares about health, this idea connects to my own life. It could help me stay consistent with my calories, macros and training schedule.
